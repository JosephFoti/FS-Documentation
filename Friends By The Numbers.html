Exhilarating. Awesome. Inspiring. These are just a few words which describe Friends Seminary. As a Quaker school, and as NYC’s oldest continuous coeducational school, Friends is distinguished by a legacy of intellectual vibrancy informed by Quaker values of integrity, equality, simplicity, community, service and peaceful conflict resolution. Service Learning and Public Purpose is an integral part of the culture at Friends. In 2017-2018, Upper School students collectively logged more than 7,500 volunteer hours.

Global Education Trips

Over the past five years, students and teachers participated in 12 global education immersion experiences, with countries including Peru, Nepal, Jordan, China, Germany, Israel, Morocco, South Africa, Colombia and France. In 2018-2019, students and teachers will travel to Taiwan and India, as well to the Deep South to visit four cities which played critical roles in the Civil Rights Movement.
