Trimester 1 | September 11 - November 30

Friends After Three offers three trimesters of unique after school programs that enrich a student’s day with fun and learning. Whether you are an artist or an athlete, a young chef or an emerging singer-songwriter, or just looking for a quiet space to tackle homework, F.A.T. has the perfect match for you every day of the week! Sign up is by trimester. Daily drop-ins are available. See the More Info section at the bottom of the page for the answers to all your F.A.T. questions. Select a division and a day of the week to learn about our after school programs. When you have made your selection click here to register online.

TEKintellect Amazing Machines
Grades K-2
Build exciting mechanized machines from Lego components including motors and remote-controlled infrared activated components. Models include Hummers, dune buggies, airplanes, carousels, windmills, Ferris wheels, elevators and many others. Students will learn the scientific principles underlying these machines, including centrifugal and centripetal forces, power transmission, potential and kinetic energies, torque, inertia and angular momentum. Experience hands-on, multi-sensory, fun-filled experimental learning which helps develop creativity, organization, imagination and teamwork.

Workshop Fee $400

Grades 6-7

Drop-in Info

Drop-ins are available for Early Bird, F.A.T. Activities and Extended Day.
Please send a note to your child’s teacher indicating a change in dismissal and that they will be dropping in to afterschool.
In addition to the dismissal note please fill out our drop-in request form by logging into Campium here.
This form must be filled out by 1:30 PM on the day of your drop-in. We will invoice you with the cost of the class, payment is expected within 7 days of billing. When your child arrives at FAT, we will give them the choices of that days available classes.
